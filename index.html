I grew up sketching cartoons and animals, and learned watercolour from my uncle Jack. Although I dropped out of art college and shifted to a hair and beauty apprenticeship—because let’s face it, art didn’t seem like a way to pay the bills—I never stopped creating.

Art has always been my way to process emotions and reflect the beauty I see in the natural world. I’ve explored tattoo design, woodwork, resin, and wire pendants. Now, I’m building the life I dreamed of—turning my passion into income. My work captures feelings more than just images, inspired by nature and adventure.

My work is deeply emotional, a reflection of my own healing, wonder, and connection to nature. I don't just paint pretty things, I create art that *feels*. Art that calms the chaos. Art that reminds you who you really are.

If you're someone who loves earthy tones, soft lines, natural rhythms and soul-deep inspiration, you're in the right place. These pieces are made for your home, and your heart.

Every artwork is designed to bring a moment of peace, reflection, and beauty into your space. Whether you're gifting or decorating, I hope you find a piece that speaks to you.
