Art that Calms the Chaos

Earthy, emotional, alchemy-led art inspired by nature, spirit and wonder. From watercolour to woodcarvings find something beautiful for you or your home!

I grew up sketching cartoons and animals, and learned watercolour from my uncle Jack. Although I dropped out of art college, because let’s face it, art didn’t seem like a way to pay the bills—I never stopped creating. Eventually, I stopped listening to other people’s doubts and started following my own path.

Now I sell my art and handmade creations, because I believe art is life's beauty — and everyone deserves something beautiful in their lives.

My work is deeply emotional, a reflection of my own healing, wonder, and connection to nature. I don't just paint pretty things — I create art that *feels*. Art that calms the chaos. Art that reminds you who you really are.

If you're someone who loves earthy tones, soft lines, natural rhythms and soul-deep inspiration, you're in the right place. These pieces are made for your home, and your heart.

Every artwork is designed to bring a moment of peace, reflection, and beauty into your space. Whether you're gifting or decorating, I hope you find a piece that speaks to you.
